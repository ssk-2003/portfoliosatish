My Projects

INTELLIGENT THREAT DETECTION IN CPS-IOT NETWORKS USING A HYBRID CNN-DBN MODEL WITH SAEHO OPTIMIZATION

Cyber-Physical Systems (CPS) and the Internet of Things (IoT) are highly interconnected environments where physical processes and computational elements interact in real-time. These systems are increasingly vulnerable to sophisticated cyber threats due to their complexity, heterogeneity, and large-scale deployment.To enhance security, this research introduces an intelligent threat detection framework that leverages a hybrid deep learning model, combining Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) and Deep Belief Networks (DBN). The model is further optimized using the Self-Adaptive Elephant Herding Optimization (SAEHO) algorithm to improve detection accuracy, adaptability, and training efficiency.
